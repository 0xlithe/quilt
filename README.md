## quilt

quilt is a focused automation workspace for x. it is designed to make repetitive engagement workflows feel controlled, measured, and intentional, with a cleaner product experience than a loose collection of scripts or one-off browser hacks.

## what quilt does

quilt brings task-based automation into a more structured surface. it is built around follow and like workflows, with human-tuned timing, session controls, and a lightweight operating model that stays close to the live x interface.

the product direction is simple: keep the experience fast, minimal, and calm while building in the safeguards, pacing, and task control that make long-running automation more usable.

## current status

quilt is in active development. the core extension architecture, popup controls, safety primitives, and dom automation foundation are in place, and the project is currently being refined toward a more reliable production-quality follow flow.

the broader goal is to shape quilt into a polished x automation product with a strong operating surface, disciplined timing behavior, and a cleaner overall user experience.
